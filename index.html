DIT Pay Processing

Input data into payroll system, calculate gross pay and deductions

Cross-Checking

DIT Editing Section and FPR Groups verify Trial Pay, Paylist accuracy

Create Banklist for banking transactions

Key Responsibilities

  • DIT Role: Primary data processing, system management, and initial quality checks
  • FPR Group Role: Data compilation, Secondary verification, approval workflow, and final sign-off
  • Joint Responsibilities: Cross-verification of Trial pay, Paylist and banklist for accuracy
  • Quality Assurance: Multi-level checking to ensure zero-error processing
Critical Success Factor: The collaboration between DIT and FPR ensures robust error detection and maintains the integrity of our payroll data and system.
